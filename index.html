What Happens During a Professional Car Service?

Initial Vehicle Inspection

The service begins with a comprehensive inspection to assess your car’s overall condition. This includes checking for warning lights, fluid leaks, unusual noises, and visible wear.

Engine Oil and Filter Change

Fresh engine oil is vital in Dubai’s climate. Technicians replace old oil and filters to:

  • Reduce engine friction

  • Prevent overheating

  • Improve engine longevity

Fluid Level Checks and Top-Ups

Your vehicle relies on multiple fluids to function smoothly, including:

  • Coolant

  • Brake fluid

  • Transmission fluid

  • Power steering fluid

  • Windshield washer fluid

Low or contaminated fluids are identified and addressed immediately.

Detailed Safety and Performance Checks

Brake System Inspection

Brakes are critical for safety, especially in high-speed conditions. A professional car service includes:

  • Brake pad and disc inspection

  • Brake fluid condition check

  • Parking brake adjustment

Battery and Electrical System Test

Dubai heat can shorten battery life. Technicians test:

  • Battery health and voltage

  • Alternator performance

  • Starter motor function

Tyres, Suspension, and Steering

Proper handling depends on these components. The service covers:

  • Tyre tread depth and pressure

  • Wheel alignment indicators

  • Suspension joints and shock absorbers

  • Steering responsiveness

Advanced Diagnostics and Computer Scanning

Modern vehicles rely heavily on onboard computers. Professional workshops use advanced diagnostic tools to:

  • Scan for hidden fault codes

  • Identify sensor or software issues

  • Reset service reminders

This step is especially important for European and luxury vehicles and is often linked with brand-specific service expertise such as those used for premium car service categories.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How often should I service my car in Dubai?

Most cars should be serviced every 10,000 km or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Luxury and high-performance vehicles may require more frequent checks.

Is professional car servicing expensive in Dubai?

Costs vary based on vehicle type and service level, but regular maintenance is far more affordable than major repairs caused by neglect.

Can I wait at the service center during my car service?

Yes, many service centers offer waiting lounges, especially for minor services.

Do professional services use genuine parts?

Reputable workshops use genuine or OEM-approved parts to ensure performance and warranty compliance.

Is a computer diagnostic scan really necessary?

Absolutely. Modern cars rely on electronics, and diagnostics help identify issues not visible during manual inspections.
